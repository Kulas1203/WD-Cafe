Our Story

More than coffee —
it's a feeling.

WD Cafe began with a simple idea: build a space where good coffee and good people meet. We source our beans directly from small farms, roast them in-house, and serve them with the kind of care that turns first-timers into regulars.

  • Ethically sourced

    Direct-trade beans from farms we know by name.

  • Baked fresh daily

    Croissants, banana bread & cookies, made each morning.

  • Made with heart

    A welcoming space where everyone's a regular.
